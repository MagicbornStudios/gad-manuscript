# Register Fragment — House Gate, Tarro

*Water-stained leaf from the gate ledger, year illegible.*

---

**To them that would descend**

You will not pass the first step as a hero passes a tavern door. You will pass as a debtor passes a toll — **named**, **witnessed**, and **stamped**.

The House of Small Mercies keeps the **registry** for those who claim business in the **Pantheon’s wound** beneath our city. We do not sell courage. We sell **order**: who went in, who swore what, who owes the choir if they emerge changed.

If you carry **charged** iron, declare it. If you carry **hope**, keep it quiet; hope makes liars of honest men.

The gate hall smells of **beer** (adventurers drink before they lie) and **smoke** (clerks burn bad paper rather than let it breed rumor). That smell is not profanity. It is **Tarro** — a kingdom that learned to treat fear like trade.

Sign here. Initial here. Swear you will not **speak** what you cannot **unhear**.

— *clerk’s hand, unreadable*
