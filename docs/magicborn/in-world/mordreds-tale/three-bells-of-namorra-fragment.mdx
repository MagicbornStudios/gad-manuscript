# The Three Bells of Namorra

*In-world monastic fragment (fiction), copied by **Brother Cale of Reedhouse**.*

---

First bell: for doctrine.  
The king declared that old power was only architecture plus obedience.

Second bell: for trial.  
He opened a copied lair under the cathedral floor and fed it relic shards, blood tithe, and six borrowed children.

Third bell: for no one.  
The floor answered upward.  
The choir answered with screaming.  
The crown answered with silence because there was no crown left to wear.

Write this plainly for later kings:  
The pantheon did not lose those kingdoms by accident.  
They tested the edge and found the edge tested back.
