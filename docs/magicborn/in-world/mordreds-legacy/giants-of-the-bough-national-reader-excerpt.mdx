## Attestation preamble (student copy)

You are not asked to **believe** a myth. You are asked to **know** what the kingdom survived when the Tree remembered it was not a metaphor.

**Two** shapes descended the trunk after the first coalition seating — not gods, **giants**. Streets ceased to be streets. The crown learned that **open air** could carry catastrophe as easily as mercy.
