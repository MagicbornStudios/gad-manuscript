Mordred spoke simply — no sermon, no storm — because simple was the only honest shape left.

"We will not pretend power is pure," he said.

"We will not pretend hunger is a virtue."

"We will **train** hands. We will **answer** for mistakes. We will **share** the bench — or we will deserve what the Tree does when nobody prunes it."

Silence — then noise — then the sound of a kingdom trying to believe it could live under **accountability**.

Aldara's eyes shone.

Rhys coughed like a man hiding emotion.

Enid nodded once — **truce**, not forgiveness.

Gwyneth mouthed a word that might have been **lesson**.
