From the highest balcony, the **Tree** looked like a wound stitched with green.

It had grown wild since the core opened — not apocalypse yet, **hunger**.

Workers swarmed scaffolding. **Magicborn** teams practiced **pruning** spells in controlled bursts.

Gwyneth pressed her palms to the rail. "It’s beautiful."

"It eats," Enid said.

Mordred nodded.

Beauty and appetite shared a face in Tarro now.

He felt **gravity** settle in his bones — his specialty, his discipline — and promised himself he would not confuse **lift** with **flight**.
