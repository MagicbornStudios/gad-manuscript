Morning light hit the **table** harder than it hit any throne.

**Mordred** stood while the choir sang something that tried to be **celebration** and kept sounding like **warning**.

He wore no crown yet — **coalition** first, vanity later.

**Enid** watched from the second row, **Gwyneth** beside her, both of them in colors that no longer meant **enemy**.

**Rhys** and **Aldara** stood for the south — tired, upright, **real**.

The **church** held the book.

The **law** held the pen.

Tarro held its breath.
