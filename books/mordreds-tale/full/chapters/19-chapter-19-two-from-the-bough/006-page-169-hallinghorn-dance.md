That evening, **Rhys** taught the hall a southern dance — hands linked, steps quick, laughter a kind of armor.

**Aldara** pulled Mordred in when he tried to refuse.

"You're allowed joy," she said.

Joy felt like a trap.

It also felt like **proof** — that war could end without turning every survivor into stone.

Gwyneth danced with a boy who wasn't a soldier yet.

Enid watched, then joined, then stopped watching herself like a weapon.

For a minute, the coalition was not policy.

It was **music**.
