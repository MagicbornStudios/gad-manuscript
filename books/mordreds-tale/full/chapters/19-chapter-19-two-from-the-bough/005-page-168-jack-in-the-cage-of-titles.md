They let **Jack** watch the ceremony from a distance — not honored, **contained**.

His smile remained.

Mordred did not go to him.

Some conversations required a bench and witnesses.

Some required a **cell**.

Jack's eyes followed Mordred the way a man follows a debt he cannot pay.

Mordred still did not know the word **father** in Jack's mouth.

He knew **patron**.

He knew **throat**.

That was enough danger for one life.
