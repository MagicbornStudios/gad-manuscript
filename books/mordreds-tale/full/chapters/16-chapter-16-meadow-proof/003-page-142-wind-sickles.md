Enid spoke in **weird speech** — not church Latin, not market Tarro — something that sounded like **kin** and **knife** at once.

**Wind sickles** cut the air.

Mordred **deflected**, **dropped**, **rolled**, and tasted grass and blood.

Gwyneth tried to flank him like a soldier.

He moved like a storm.

He hated how easy it was.

He hated that part of him still wanted to **show** them — *see? see what I can do?* — the child hunger of a boy who had been named **devil** before he was named **son**.
