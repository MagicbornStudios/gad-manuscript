They met at mid-morning when the grass still held dew — **meadow** country honest about blood.

**Enid** dismounted.

**Gwyneth** stayed mounted a heartbeat longer, then slid down, boots firm, soldier-shaped.

Mordred walked forward until the space between them felt like a chapel — quiet, watched, **too** watched.

Jack's army breathed on one side.

Hallinghorn and southern allies breathed on the other.

Rhys muttered a prayer that sounded like a curse.

Aldara's hand hovered near bandages she hoped not to need.
