Gwyneth fell hard, breath ragged, eyes still **hungry**.

"Teach me," she gasped — not surrender, **need**.

Mordred looked at her and saw **Jack** in the shape of her jaw, not because he knew Jack was her father — he didn't — but because the world kept rhyming.

Enid knelt beside her daughter, shielding, furious, **alive**.

"Terms," Enid said.

Mordred nodded.

That was how wars ended — not with silence, with **words** you could carry.
