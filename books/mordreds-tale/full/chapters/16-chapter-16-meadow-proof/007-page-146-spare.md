Mordred let a unit retreat — Jack's colors, broken — **on purpose**.

Rhys watched him do it and smiled like a man who had just seen his investment pay out.

**Coalition seed** — not friendship, **future**.

Aldara bound Enid's arm without asking permission.

Enid let her.

The meadow smelled like scorched grass and copper.

Mordred's broken arm sang.

He had won.

He did not feel clean.
