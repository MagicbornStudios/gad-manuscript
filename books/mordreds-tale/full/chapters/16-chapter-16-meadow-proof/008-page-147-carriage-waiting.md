Jack's castle waited — **not** far now — a stone throat.

They would ride toward it under truce flags, enemies and allies sharing a road like a bad joke.

Gwyneth's eyes never left Mordred.

Enid's eyes never left **Jack**.

Mordred's eyes never left the horizon — where the **Tree** still grew, where the world still felt **new**.

The carriage was a promise.

**Terms** inside.

Whatever Jack was, whatever Morgana was, whatever **father** meant — the next chapter would be spoken in a closed box, wheels turning, **truth** optional.
