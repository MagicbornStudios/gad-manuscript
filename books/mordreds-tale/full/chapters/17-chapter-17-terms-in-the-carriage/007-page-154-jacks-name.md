Through the carriage wall, they heard **Jack**'s horse — close, controlled.

Enid went still.

Gwyneth's fingers dug into her own palm.

Mordred felt the name **Jack** like weather — pressure, humidity, old fear.

He did not think **father**.

He thought **patron**.

He thought **throat**.

Whatever truth the carriage would force next, it would not be gentle.

The wheels turned.

The castle rose ahead — stone teeth — and Tarro prepared to swallow another chapter.
