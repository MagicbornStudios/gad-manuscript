As the gates opened, Mordred looked up at the banners and felt small on purpose.

Small kept you honest.

He stepped out into wind that tasted like **tree** and **empire** and **new law**.

Rhys clapped him once on the back — hard, grounding.

Aldara nodded — healer approval, soldier worry.

Inside, the **bench** waited: not a throne, a **table**.

If they survived the signing, the war would end in paperwork.

If they survived the signing, the **real** war — how to live with power — would begin.

Mordred walked forward anyway.
