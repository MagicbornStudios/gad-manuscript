They wrote **terms** by lamplight — not final law, **shape**:

- a **coalition bench**
- southern voices with real weight
- Enid and Gwyneth **inside** the order, not hung outside as trophies
- Jack's fate **unsettled** — prison, exile, **cage** — Mordred's pen hovering

Mordred's hand shook once.

Not from fear.

From understanding that **mercy** and **politics** could share a desk.

Enid looked at him.

"If you make me a saint," she said, "I'll bite you."

Mordred snorted — unexpected laughter, relief.

Gwyneth smiled like a girl who had just learned a new kind of power.
