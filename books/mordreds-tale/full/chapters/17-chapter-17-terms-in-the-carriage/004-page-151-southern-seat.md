**Rhys** rode alongside long enough to shout through the window — irreverent, alive.

"Aldara says if you sign anything without us, she'll break your good arm too."

Mordred almost smiled.

**Southern allies** weren't decoration in this version of the story — they were **bench** stakes.

Enid nodded, grim respect.

Gwyneth looked curious, as if war had been a lesson and politics might be another.

The road unspooled toward **Tarro**'s heart.

Jack waited.

So did the church.

So did the question of what **Magicborn** would mean when it stopped being rumor and became **law**.
