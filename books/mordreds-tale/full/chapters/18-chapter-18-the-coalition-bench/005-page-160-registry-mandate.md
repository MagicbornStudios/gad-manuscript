They signed the **Magicborn registry** into law — not as cruelty, as **duty**:

track power,

train hands,

send rotas down into the **stump** when the Tree tried to eat streets.

Mordred's pen shook.

He signed anyway.

**Aldara** whispered, "We can amend what we survive."

**Rhys** grinned. "Cheerful."

**Gwyneth** watched the ink dry like it was a spell itself.

Outside, Tarro breathed new weather.

Inside, they tried to make weather **accountable**.
