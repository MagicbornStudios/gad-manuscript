Someone asked about the woman under the castle.

The room went quiet.

**Enid**'s face hardened.

Mordred's hands tightened.

The church elder murmured about **mercy** and **security** and **state**.

Rhys said, "If she's a prisoner, she's a **fact**. Facts on paper or facts in blood — pick."

They picked **paper** — for now.

Not freedom.

Not yet.

A line item: **review**.

A promise: **later**.

Mordred swallowed rage until it sat in his stomach like a stone.

He would come back for **names** — his mother's, his own — when the bench could bear them.
