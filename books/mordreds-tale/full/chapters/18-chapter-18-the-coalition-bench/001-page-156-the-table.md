The **bench** was oak, scarred, long enough for enemies to sit without pretending to be friends.

**Mordred** took the head because they made him — crown optional, power not.

**Enid** sat left. **Gwyneth** behind her like an honor guard and a threat.

**Rhys** and **Aldara** sat for **Hallinghorn** and the southern coalition — names finally **weight**, not rumor.

The **church** brought ink and **sealed-tongue** witnesses.

**Jack** was brought in last — **alive**, bound by politics more than rope — and the room inhaled.

Morgana was not here.

Morgana was still a secret the stone kept.
