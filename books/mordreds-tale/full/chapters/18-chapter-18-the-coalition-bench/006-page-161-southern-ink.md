Hallinghorn's seal went down beside Tarro's — not submission, **alliance**.

Rhys exhaled like he'd been holding air since childhood.

Aldara's hand rested on the table after — flat, steady — a healer's promise that the body politic would need stitching for years.

Enid looked at Mordred.

"If you break this," she said, "I'll come for you."

Mordred nodded.

He wanted someone to come for him if he turned into a throat.

That was love in their language.
