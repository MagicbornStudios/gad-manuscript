That night, Mordred walked the castle wall and felt **gravity** answer calmly — no surge, no fling — just **him**, just **practice**.

His arm ached, healed wrong until healers set it again.

The world felt **new** and **old** at once.

Somewhere, a scribe copied the new law into a **national reader** series — training texts for the ones who would hold the dungeon line.

Somewhere else, a tree **grew** faster than scaffolding.

The coalition had bought time.

Time, Mordred knew, was where the next story always began.
