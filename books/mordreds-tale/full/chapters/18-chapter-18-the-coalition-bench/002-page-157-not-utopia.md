Nobody pretended the peace would be pure.

**Tarro** wanted the gate.

**Hallinghorn** wanted breath.

The **church** wanted souls manageable.

**Enid** wanted survival with dignity.

**Gwyneth** wanted **knowledge**.

Mordred wanted to not become the next throat.

They argued until the candles spat and the scribes' hands cramped — **tax**, **duty rotas**, **dungeon** sorties, who fed the poor when magic made hunger **strange**.

It was ugly.

It was **real**.

That was the coalition difference.
