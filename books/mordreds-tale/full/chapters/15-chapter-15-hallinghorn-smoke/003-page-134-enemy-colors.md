They saw her at dawn — **Enid** on a tall horse, hair braided for war, **Jack's** seal on her cloak like a bruise.

**Gwyneth** rode shorter, older than her years, eyes everywhere.

Mordred's mouth went dry.

Not because he hated them.

Because he recognized them — the way you recognize weather you've survived once already.

Enid lifted her hand.

Not a wave.

A **warning**.

The southern line shifted, shields up, Rhys shouting something that sounded like a joke and functioned like an order.

Magic sparked along grass — **new** magic, **wild** magic — and the meadow learned what it meant to be a battlefield again.
