Tarro's wealth had always been a **choke**.

Relics. Charge. Permission to descend.

Now magic ran free — and the choke became a **flood**. Kingdoms panicked. Markets screamed. The church tried to own the sky with paperwork.

Rhys laughed without humor. "They're going to tax **breath** next."

Aldara's mouth tightened. "They already do. They call it **registry**."

Mordred thought of the House, of **Merris**, of bark bands snapped like courtesy.

He thought: *I will not build another cage.*

He knew, even as he thought it, that cages could be built with good intentions — **coalition**, **safety**, **duty**.

The southern camp smelled like cider and sweat and honest fear.

It was the first place that felt like a **maybe**.
